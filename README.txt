Nameful Team presents:

TWIRIO

What's going on? One day, Twirio finds an impassable obstacle.
But, as he gains a new ability, the world will gain an entirely new dimension.

Side Scroll Controls:
Left/Right: Move
Up: Jump
Q: Change The World

Top Down Controls:

Left/Right/Up/Down: Move
Space: Use Weapon
Q: Change The World

Gamepad:
Use B instead of Space, X instead of Q.
